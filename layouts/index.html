Building the open data center.

Bringing hyperscale agility and velocity to enterprise

The hyperscalers have internally been building their own custom hardware for the benefits of scale, economics, power efficiency, reliability, and security. We are building a product for enterprises to do the same.

Rack-scale machines

Our product will be a whole rack -- no more, no less. This will expose an API for managing compute and a Dashboard for tracking inventory, capacity, licenses, etc.

Ensure the integrity of your infrastructure

Vulnerabilities in the lower layers of the stack are a threat to anyone operating a data center. We are building a root of trust to ensure the integrity of the hardware and software components are in tact before any workload is deployed.

Lower your power consumption costs

The result from Facebook switching to their Open Compute Project hardware was 38% percent less energy to do the same work as their existing facilities. It was a cost savings of 24%.
